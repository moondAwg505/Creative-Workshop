About

“Creative Workshop” opened its doors in the early 2000s to give your favorite belongings a second life. The workshop is run by an experienced craftsman skilled in shoe repair, key cutting, and tool sharpening. In our atelier, a skilled artisan specializes in clothing repair and tailoring, bringing your ideas to life. “Creative Workshop” is a place where quality, craftsmanship, and a personalized approach to each customer are valued. We uphold the traditions of mastery, offering modern solutions for repairing and restoring your items.

Services

Master’s Services:

  1. Key Cutting
  2. Household Tool Sharpening
  3. Shoe and Bag Repair

Atelier Services:

  1. Clothing and Bag Repair and Tailoring
  2. Car Seat Cover Repair
  3. Handmade Knitwear
